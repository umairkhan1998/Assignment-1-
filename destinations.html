Nestled in the Margalla Range near Islamabad, Pakistan, Murree is a captivating hill station that offers a quick escape from the bustle of city life.

Day 1: Arrival

Arrive in Murree and check into your cozy mountain lodge. Breathe in the crisp mountain air and unwind by the fireplace.


Day 2: Explore

Visit Pindi Point for stunning views of Rawalpindi and stroll along Mall Road for local treats and shopping.


Day 3: Nature

Discover Patriata and the exhilarating cable car rides, then explore the beautiful Peshawar Mosque.


Day 4: Adventure

Get your adrenaline pumping with a day of trekking and hiking in the breathtaking Margalla Range.


Day 5: Departure

Take a final walk along Mall Road, collect souvenirs, and savor your last cup of Pakistani tea before bidding farewell to this tranquil haven. In just a few days, Murree offers an unforgettable blend of natural beauty and culture.
